Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 8, 2026

Kindergarten Learning Adventure is a fun, educational app designed for children ages 4–6. The app is completely offline and runs entirely on the user’s device.

We collect no personal information whatsoever.

This app is intended solely for children under the age of 13 and fully complies with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. Because the app is 100% offline and self-contained, no parental consent is required.

Sharing of Information

We do not share, sell, or disclose any data to third parties because we do not collect any data.

Data Security

All information stays on the user’s device and is protected by the device’s built-in security features.
